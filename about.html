About ZeroSphere

Innovation for a cleaner, data-driven future.

ZeroSphere is an initiative focused on measuring, analyzing, and reducing carbon emissions from coal mines using technology and transparency.

Our Vision

ZeroSphere is designed to simplify complex emission calculations and present actionable insights for industries. The platform brings together sustainability, data analytics, and awareness to help mining companies understand their carbon footprint and move toward a greener operational model.
